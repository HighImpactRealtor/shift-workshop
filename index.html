Your Guide

Jeremy Larkin

I've been where you are.

I went from 6 transactions in year one — with three little kids at home and no idea what I was doing — to building an operation that consistently closed 100+ deals a year.

And for a long time, I thought that was the goal. More deals. More revenue. More. But the business I built required all of me to run. I was the engine. And I was exhausted.

What changed everything wasn't a tactic or a script. It was an identity shift.

I stopped being the producer who happened to be good at sales and became the operator who built something that runs without me at the center of every transaction. That shift is what this workshop is about.

Last year, I personally closed 25 deals, led our team to $100M, and I honeymooned, took 25 ski days, and was home by 5:30 (or much earlier) almost every day.

This is the conversation I wish someone had given me fifteen years ago. Just the real talk about what has to change — and why most Agents avoid it.

— Jeremy
