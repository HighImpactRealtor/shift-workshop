Is This You?

The business is working. You are too.

  • You closed 20, 40, maybe 100+ deals last year — and somehow you're more stressed than when you started.
  • Your brain never fully shuts off. You're home, but you're not really home. Your phone is always in your hand.
  • You've broken a promise to yourself — or your spouse — about how much you were going to work this year. Maybe more than once.
  • You got here on grit and hustle. And you have no idea how to grow without more of it.
  • Something is starting to cost you that you can't quite name yet — but you can feel it.
  • You don't have a business problem. You have an identity problem. The skills that built this are the same ones keeping you trapped in it.

If you're nodding right now — this workshop was built for you.

Your Guide

Jeremy Larkin

21-Year Real Estate Veteran · 2,200+ Sales · St. George, Utah

I went from 6 transactions in year one — with three little kids at home and no idea what I was doing — to building a team that consistently closed 100+ deals a year.

And for a long time, I thought that was the goal. More deals. More revenue. More. But the business I built required all of me to run. I was the engine. And I was exhausted.

What changed everything wasn't a tactic or a script. It was an identity shift. I stopped being the producer who happened to have a team, and I became the operator who happened to be great at sales. That shift is what this workshop is about.

This is the conversation I wish someone had given me fifteen years ago. No pitch. No fluff. Just the real talk about what has to change — and why most agents avoid it.

— Jeremy
